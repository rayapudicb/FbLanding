Brand Guidelines

How to properly represent the letsflick brand

Our Brand Story

letsflick represents authenticity in motion. We believe that genuine human connections happen through unfiltered, spontaneous video conversations. Our brand embodies the spirit of real expression, meaningful dialogue, and community building through the power of video threading.

Brand Mission

To enable authentic self-expression through video threads that drive real conversations and build genuine communities.

Brand Personality

Brand Attributes

  • Authentic: We celebrate realness over perfection
  • Connected: We bring people together through meaningful conversations
  • Innovative: We pioneer new ways to communicate and express
  • Inclusive: We welcome all voices and perspectives
  • Energetic: We inspire action and engagement

Brand Voice

  • Conversational: We speak like a friend, not a corporation
  • Encouraging: We inspire people to be their authentic selves
  • Clear: We communicate simply and directly
  • Positive: We focus on possibilities and connections
  • Human: We acknowledge emotions and real experiences

Brand Tone Guidelines

Do Use:

  • Warm, welcoming language that makes everyone feel included
  • Active voice and direct communication
  • Conversational contractions (we're, you'll, let's)
  • Encouraging phrases that inspire action
  • Language that celebrates authenticity and real moments

Avoid:

  • Corporate jargon or overly technical language
  • Pretentious or exclusionary terminology
  • Negative or discouraging messaging
  • Overpromising or exaggerated claims
  • Language that promotes superficiality or inauthenticity

Logo Guidelines

Primary Logo

The letsflick wordmark is our primary logo. It should be used in most applications and contexts.

Logo Variations

Light Backgrounds

Use the golden yellow version (#F1C232) on light backgrounds for maximum visibility and brand recognition.

Dark Backgrounds

letsflick

Dark Background Version - White (#FFFFFF)

Monochrome Applications

For single-color applications, use solid black on light backgrounds or white on dark backgrounds.

Logo Usage Rules

Do:

  • Maintain the logo's original proportions
  • Provide adequate clear space around the logo
  • Use approved color variations only
  • Ensure the logo is legible at all sizes
  • Use high-resolution versions for print materials

Don't:

  • Stretch, compress, or distort the logo
  • Change the font or letter spacing
  • Add effects, shadows, or outlines
  • Use unauthorized colors
  • Place the logo on busy or conflicting backgrounds
  • Make the logo smaller than the minimum size requirement

Minimum Size and Clear Space

  • Minimum Size: 24px height for digital, 0.5 inches for print
  • Clear Space: Maintain space equal to the height of the "l" character on all sides
  • Maximum Size: No larger than 20% of the total design area

Typography

Primary Typeface

Inter

Inter is our primary typeface for all digital applications. It provides excellent readability and a modern, clean aesthetic that aligns with our brand personality.

Inter Bold
Inter Semi-Bold
Inter Medium
Inter Regular
Inter Light

Typography Hierarchy

  • Headlines: Inter Bold, 32-72px depending on context
  • Subheadlines: Inter Semi-Bold, 24-32px
  • Body Text: Inter Regular, 16-18px
  • Captions: Inter Medium, 14-16px
  • Small Text: Inter Regular, 12-14px

Fallback Fonts

When Inter is not available, use the following system font stack:

  • -apple-system (iOS/macOS)
  • BlinkMacSystemFont (macOS)
  • 'Segoe UI' (Windows)
  • system-ui (Modern browsers)
  • sans-serif (Universal fallback)

Visual Style

Photography Style

  • Authentic: Real people in genuine moments, not overly staged
  • Bright: Well-lit images with natural lighting when possible
  • Diverse: Representing the full spectrum of our community
  • Engaging: Images that tell a story and evoke emotion
  • High Quality: Sharp, professional imagery with good composition

Illustration Style

  • Simple: Clean lines and minimal complexity
  • Friendly: Approachable and warm visual elements
  • Consistent: Uniform style across all illustrations
  • Purposeful: Each illustration should serve a clear communication goal

Video Guidelines

  • Vertical Format: 9:16 aspect ratio for mobile-first experience
  • Natural Sound: Authentic audio without excessive processing
  • Good Lighting: Clear, well-lit subjects
  • Steady Footage: Stable recording without excessive camera shake
  • Authentic Moments: Genuine expressions and interactions

Brand Compliance

Usage Approval

While we encourage proper use of our brand materials, certain uses require approval:

  • Commercial usage outside of official partnerships
  • Modifications to our logo or brand elements
  • Integration with other brand logos or elements
  • Large-scale promotional or marketing campaigns
  • Any usage that could be considered endorsement

Prohibited Uses

  • Using our brand to promote competing products or services
  • Modifying our logo, colors, or brand elements
  • Suggesting official partnership without authorization
  • Using our brand in connection with inappropriate content
  • Creating derivative works based on our brand assets

Enforcement

We actively monitor usage of our brand assets and will take action against:

  • Unauthorized commercial usage
  • Brand misuse that could cause confusion
  • Usage that damages our brand reputation
  • Trademark infringement or counterfeiting
