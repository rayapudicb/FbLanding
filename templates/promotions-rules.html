Promotions Rules

Guidelines for contests, giveaways, and promotional campaigns

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

These rules govern all promotional activities on letsflick, including contests, sweepstakes, giveaways, challenges, and other promotional campaigns. All promotions must comply with these rules and applicable laws.

Our Promotion Philosophy

We believe promotions should be fun, fair, and accessible to our community while maintaining the authentic spirit of letsflick.

Types of Promotions

Platform Promotions

Official letsflick promotions run by our team:

  • Community Challenges: Platform-wide video threading challenges
  • Feature Launch Contests: Promotions celebrating new features
  • Seasonal Campaigns: Holiday and special occasion promotions
  • Creator Spotlights: Contests highlighting exceptional creators
  • User Milestone Celebrations: Promotions marking platform milestones

Brand-Sponsored Promotions

Promotional campaigns sponsored by third-party brands:

  • Branded Challenges: Hashtag campaigns with prizes
  • Product Launch Contests: Promotions introducing new products
  • Collaboration Campaigns: Creator and brand partnership promotions
  • Seasonal Sponsorships: Holiday or event-themed brand promotions

Creator-Led Promotions

Promotional activities organized by individual creators:

  • Personal Giveaways: Creators giving away their own prizes
  • Milestone Celebrations: Promotions celebrating follower milestones
  • Collaboration Contests: Multi-creator promotional campaigns
  • Fan Engagement: Promotions to build creator-audience relationships

General Promotion Requirements

Legal Compliance

  • All promotions must comply with local, state, and federal laws
  • International promotions must comply with laws in all participating jurisdictions
  • Proper licenses and permits must be obtained where required
  • Tax obligations must be clearly communicated to winners
  • Age restrictions must be clearly stated and enforced

Official Rules Requirements

All promotions must include complete official rules containing:

  • Eligibility Requirements: Who can participate
  • Entry Periods: Start and end dates/times with time zone
  • How to Enter: Clear entry instructions
  • Prize Descriptions: Detailed prize information and values
  • Winner Selection: How winners will be chosen
  • Notification Process: How winners will be contacted
  • Terms and Conditions: Additional restrictions and requirements

Disclosure Requirements

  • Clear identification of promotion sponsor
  • Statement that letsflick is not the sponsor (for non-platform promotions)
  • Full terms and conditions easily accessible
  • Prize details including approximate retail value
  • Any limitations or restrictions clearly stated

Platform Promotion Guidelines

Official letsflick Promotions

When letsflick runs official promotions, we follow these standards:

Eligibility Standards

  • Open to legal residents of participating countries
  • Minimum age requirement (typically 13+ with parental consent)
  • Valid letsflick account in good standing required
  • Employees and immediate family members excluded
  • One entry per person unless otherwise specified

Prize Fulfillment

  • Prizes will be awarded as described in official rules
  • Winners responsible for applicable taxes
  • Prize substitution may occur if necessary
  • Unclaimed prizes may be forfeited after specified period
  • Winners may be required to sign publicity and tax forms

Winner Selection

  • Random drawings conducted by independent third parties when applicable
  • Judged contests evaluated by qualified panels
  • Selection criteria clearly communicated in advance
  • Winner verification process to confirm eligibility
  • Alternate winners selected if primary winners are disqualified

Third-Party Promotion Guidelines

Brand-Sponsored Promotions

Pre-Approval Requirements

  • All brand-sponsored promotions require platform approval
  • Submit promotion details at least 14 days before launch
  • Provide complete official rules and legal documentation
  • Demonstrate compliance with advertising policies
  • Agree to promotion monitoring and reporting requirements

Content Requirements

  • Clear disclosure of sponsorship and prize source
  • Compliance with community guidelines and advertising policies
  • Appropriate content for letsflick's diverse audience
  • Authentic integration with platform culture and features
  • No misleading claims or unrealistic promises

Prize and Fulfillment Standards

  • Sponsors responsible for all prize fulfillment
  • Prize values must be accurately represented
  • Winners contacted directly by sponsor or authorized agent
  • Dispute resolution handled by sponsor according to official rules
  • Platform may request proof of prize fulfillment

Creator-Led Promotions

Creator Responsibilities

  • Creators fully responsible for their promotional activities
  • Must comply with FTC guidelines for endorsements and giveaways
  • Clear disclosure that letsflick is not sponsoring or administering
  • Responsible for all legal compliance and prize fulfillment
  • Must follow community guidelines and platform policies

Platform Support

  • letsflick does not endorse or guarantee creator promotions
  • Platform may remove promotion content that violates policies
  • Disputes between creators and participants handled independently
  • Educational resources available for promotion best practices
  • Reporting tools available for inappropriate promotional content

Prohibited Promotion Practices

Fraudulent Activities

  • Fake giveaways or contests with no intention of awarding prizes
  • Misrepresentation of prize values or availability
  • False claims about official platform endorsement
  • Manipulation of entry or selection processes
  • Use of bots or automated systems to enter contests

Inappropriate Content

  • Promotions involving illegal products or activities
  • Adult content or age-inappropriate prizes
  • Promotion of harmful or dangerous products
  • Content that violates community guidelines
  • Discriminatory promotion criteria or messaging

Misleading Practices

  • Hidden terms and conditions
  • Impossible or unreasonable entry requirements
  • Fake winner announcements
  • Bait-and-switch prize substitutions
  • Excessive data collection under guise of entry requirements

Entry and Participation Guidelines

Valid Entry Requirements

  • Account Standing: Active account in good standing required
  • Content Guidelines: Entry content must comply with community standards
  • Original Content: Entries must be original work (unless otherwise specified)
  • Rights Granted: Participants grant usage rights as specified in rules
  • Multiple Entries: Clearly state if multiple entries are allowed

Disqualification Criteria

  • Violation of official rules or platform policies
  • Inappropriate or offensive content
  • Use of copyrighted material without permission
  • Fraudulent or automated entry methods
  • Failure to meet eligibility requirements

User Rights and Responsibilities

  • Right to Privacy: Personal information protected according to privacy policy
  • Right to Fair Treatment: Equal opportunity for all eligible participants
  • Responsibility to Comply: Follow all rules and platform policies
  • Content Ownership: Retain ownership while granting promotional usage rights
  • Reporting Issues: Report suspicious or inappropriate promotional activity

International Promotions

Global Compliance

  • Compliance with laws in all participating jurisdictions
  • Proper registration and licensing where required
  • Cultural sensitivity and appropriate localization
  • Clear communication of geographic restrictions
  • Currency and taxation considerations

Regional Considerations

European Union

  • GDPR compliance for data collection and processing
  • Country-specific gambling and promotion regulations
  • Clear terms in local languages where required
  • Proper disclosure of commercial intent

United States

  • FTC disclosure requirements for endorsements
  • State-specific sweepstakes and contest regulations
  • Registration requirements in New York, Florida, and Rhode Island
  • Tax reporting obligations for prize values over $600

Other Regions

  • Local advertising and promotion regulations
  • Cultural and religious considerations
  • Currency exchange and prize fulfillment logistics
  • Import/export restrictions for physical prizes

Reporting and Enforcement

Reporting Violations

Users can report inappropriate promotional activities:

  • Use in-app reporting tools for suspicious contests
  • Email promotions@letsflick.com with detailed information
  • Include screenshots and relevant documentation
  • Reports reviewed within 24-48 hours
  • Action taken based on severity of violation

Platform Enforcement

Minor Violations

  • Warning and education about proper promotion practices
  • Required correction of misleading information
  • Removal of non-compliant promotional content

Serious Violations

  • Suspension of promotional privileges
  • Account restrictions or temporary suspension
  • Requirement for legal compliance verification

Severe Violations

  • Permanent ban from running promotions on platform
  • Account termination for fraudulent activities
  • Reporting to appropriate authorities for illegal activities

Frequently Asked Questions

For Brands and Marketers

Q: How far in advance should we submit promotion details?

A: We recommend submitting promotion details at least 14 days before your planned launch date to allow time for review and any necessary revisions.

Q: Can we run the same promotion on multiple social media platforms?

A: Yes, but make sure to clearly identify which platforms are included and follow each platform's specific guidelines.

Q: What happens if our promotion violates platform policies?

A: We'll work with you to bring the promotion into compliance. In severe cases, we may remove the content and restrict future promotional activities.

For Creators

Q: Do I need approval to run a personal giveaway?

A: No prior approval is needed for personal giveaways, but you must comply with all applicable laws and clearly state that letsflick is not the sponsor.

Q: Can I require followers to share personal information to enter?

A: You should limit data collection to what's necessary for prize fulfillment and comply with privacy laws. Never request sensitive information like passwords or financial data.

Q: What if I can't fulfill a prize I promised?

A: You're legally obligated to fulfill promised prizes. If circumstances prevent this, consult with a legal professional about your options and obligations.

For Participants

Q: How do I know if a promotion is legitimate?

A: Look for complete official rules, clear prize descriptions, and proper sponsor identification. Be wary of promotions requiring payment or sensitive personal information.

Q: What should I do if I win but don't receive my prize?

A: First contact the promotion sponsor directly. If that doesn't resolve the issue, you can report it to us and may need to seek legal advice for valuable prizes.

Q: Can I enter the same contest multiple times?

A: Only if the official rules specifically allow multiple entries. Creating fake accounts to enter multiple times is prohibited.
