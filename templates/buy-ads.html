Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I start advertising?

Self-service campaigns can launch within 24 hours. Managed campaigns typically launch within 1-2 weeks, and enterprise solutions may take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity.

What creative formats work best?

Authentic, vertical videos (9:16 aspect ratio) perform best on our platform. We recommend keeping content under 10 seconds with clear branding and compelling calls-to-action.

How do you ensure brand safety?

We use advanced AI content moderation, manual review processes, and give advertisers full control over content adjacency to ensure your brand appears in appropriate contexts.

Can I target specific demographics?

Yes, we offer detailed targeting options including age, gender, location, interests, and behaviors. Enterprise clients can also upload custom audience lists.

What kind of results should I expect?

Performance varies by industry and campaign type, but typical results include 2-5% engagement rates, 15-30% higher brand recall than traditional social ads, and strong conversion rates for well-targeted campaigns.
