Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

New to letsflick? Here's everything you need to know to start creating video threads and connecting with others.

How do I create my first video thread?

Creating your first video thread is simple:

  1. Open the letsflick app on your mobile device
  2. Tap the camera icon to start recording
  3. Record a 10-second selfie video
  4. Add a caption or hashtags if desired
  5. Share your video thread with the community

How do I reply to someone's video thread?

Engaging with other users' content is what makes letsflick special:

  • Find a video thread you want to respond to
  • Tap the "Reply" button
  • Record your 10-second response video
  • Your reply will be added to the thread conversation

What makes a good video thread?

The best video threads on letsflick are authentic, engaging, and spark meaningful conversations. Here are some tips:

  • Be yourself - authenticity resonates with viewers
  • Ask questions to encourage responses
  • Share personal experiences or perspectives
  • Keep your videos clear and well-lit
  • Engage with replies to your threads

Technical Support

App Performance Issues

If you're experiencing slow loading times or crashes:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  • Restart the app and try again
  • Check your internet connection
  • Clear the app cache if problems persist

Video Upload Problems

Having trouble uploading your videos? Here are some solutions:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Check that your device has enough storage space
  • Videos should be 10 seconds or less
  • Try recording in a different location if upload fails

Account & Privacy

For account-related questions:

  • Update your privacy settings in the app menu
  • Report inappropriate content using the flag button
  • Block users who violate community guidelines
  • Delete your account in Settings > Account > Delete Account

Contact Our Support Team

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.

Get in Touch

Email us at support@letsflick.com and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

For urgent issues, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your username or email associated with your account
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Description of the issue you're experiencing
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if applicable
