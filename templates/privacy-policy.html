Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how letsflick, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our video threading platform and mobile application.

Your Privacy Matters

We're committed to transparency about how we handle your data and giving you control over your personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and phone number
  • Username and display name
  • Profile photo and bio information
  • Birth date (for age verification)
  • Account preferences and settings

Content You Create

  • Video threads you record and upload
  • Comments and replies to other videos
  • Captions, hashtags, and descriptions
  • Direct messages and communications
  • Likes, follows, and other interactions

Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and general location data
  • Usage patterns and time spent in app
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Camera and microphone access (with permission)

How We Use Your Information

To Provide Our Service

  • Enable video recording, uploading, and streaming
  • Facilitate replies and conversations
  • Maintain your account and profile
  • Process your interactions (likes, follows, comments)
  • Provide customer support

To Improve Your Experience

  • Recommend relevant video threads
  • Personalize your feed and suggestions
  • Analyze usage to improve our features
  • Develop new products and services
  • Optimize app performance

For Safety and Security

  • Monitor for policy violations
  • Detect and prevent spam or abuse
  • Investigate reported content
  • Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Comply with legal requirements

Information Sharing

Public Information

The following information is public by default:

  • Your username and profile photo
  • Public video threads and their replies
  • Your follower and following counts
  • Comments on public videos

With Other Users

  • Direct messages with users you communicate with
  • Your activity on videos (likes, replies)
  • Information in your profile (if public)

With Third Parties

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate our platform
  • Analytics Partners: For understanding app usage (anonymized data only)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Business Partners: Only with your explicit consent

We Never Sell Your Data

letsflick does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Privacy Controls

Account Settings

  • Make your account private
  • Control who can reply to your videos
  • Limit who can send you direct messages
  • Turn off location sharing
  • Disable personalized recommendations

Video Privacy

  • Set individual videos to private
  • Delete videos at any time
  • Control who can download your content
  • Limit comments and replies

Data Management

  • Download a copy of your data
  • Delete specific content or data types
  • Close your account permanently
  • Opt out of data processing for recommendations

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All video content is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security assessments and updates
  • Secure Infrastructure: Cloud providers with SOC 2 compliance

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours via email and in-app notification.

Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Video Content: Until you delete individual videos
  • Usage Data: Up to 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Support Communications: Up to 3 years for quality assurance
  • Legal Compliance: As required by applicable laws

Automatic Deletion

Some data is automatically deleted:

  • Temporary files and cache data (30 days)
  • Failed video uploads (24 hours)
  • Anonymous usage statistics (24 months)
  • Deleted account data (90 days after confirmation)

Children's Privacy

We take special care to protect children's privacy:

  • Users must be 13+ to create an account
  • Parental consent required for users under 16
  • Enhanced privacy settings for teen accounts
  • Limited data collection from underage users
  • No targeted advertising to users under 18

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at privacy@letsflick.com

International Users

Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (EU users)
  • Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)
  • Local data residency requirements
  • Cross-border data agreements

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification or correction
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Notify you via email and in-app notification
  • Post the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Highlight significant changes in plain language
  • Provide 30 days notice before major changes take effect

Your continued use of letsflick after changes become effective means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data:

  • Email: privacy@letsflick.com
  • Mail: letsflick, Inc., Privacy Team, 123 Tech Street, San Francisco, CA 94105
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@letsflick.com
