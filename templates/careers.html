Why Work at letsflick?

At letsflick, we believe that authentic connections through video can change the world. We're building a platform where people can be their genuine selves and create meaningful conversations that matter.

Our Mission

To enable authentic self-expression through video threads that drive real conversations and build genuine communities.

Our Values

Authenticity First

We value genuine connections over artificial interactions. Our team members are encouraged to bring their true selves to work and speak honestly about challenges and opportunities.

User-Centric Innovation

Every decision we make starts with our users. We innovate not for the sake of technology, but to solve real problems and create meaningful experiences.

Inclusive Community

We're building a platform for everyone, and our team reflects that diversity. We welcome people from all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives.

Sustainable Growth

We believe in growing responsibly, both as a company and as individuals. We support work-life balance and long-term career development.

Our Interview Process

Step 1: Application Review

Our recruiting team reviews all applications within one week. We look for alignment with our values, relevant experience, and passion for our mission.

Step 2: Initial Conversation (30 minutes)

A casual conversation with a member of our team to learn more about you, your background, and discuss the role in detail.

Step 3: Skills Assessment

Depending on the role, this might include:

  • Technical coding challenge or system design (Engineering)
  • Product case study or portfolio review (Product/Design)
  • Marketing campaign proposal (Marketing)
  • Written communication assessment (Various roles)

Step 4: Team Interviews (2-3 hours total)

Meet with potential teammates and collaborators. These conversations focus on:

  • Technical skills and problem-solving approach
  • Cultural fit and collaboration style
  • Experience and career goals
  • Questions about letsflick and the team

Step 5: Leadership Interview (45 minutes)

Final conversation with a member of the leadership team to discuss vision alignment, career goals, and answer any remaining questions.

Step 6: Reference Checks & Offer

We'll conduct reference checks and extend an offer if there's mutual interest. Our goal is to complete the entire process within 2-3 weeks.
