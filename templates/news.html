News & Updates

Latest announcements and product updates from letsflick

Recent News

December 15, 2024 - letsflick Launches Public Beta

We're excited to announce that letsflick is now available in public beta! After months of development and private testing, we're ready to open our doors to everyone who wants to experience authentic video conversations.

November 28, 2024 - Introducing Video Threading

Today we're announcing our core feature: Video Threading. This revolutionary approach to social media allows users to create conversation chains using 10-second selfie videos, fostering more authentic and meaningful interactions than traditional text-based platforms.

Key Features Include:

  • 10-second video format optimized for authentic expression
  • Thread-based conversations that encourage deeper engagement
  • Privacy controls that put users in control of their content
  • Community guidelines designed to promote respectful dialogue

October 20, 2024 - Series A Funding Announcement

letsflick successfully raised $15M in Series A funding led by Authentic Ventures, with participation from Video Capital and several prominent angel investors in the social media space.

The funding will be used to:

  • Expand our engineering and product teams
  • Enhance our AI-powered content moderation systems
  • Develop new features for content creators
  • Scale our infrastructure to support global growth

September 10, 2024 - Safety First Initiative

We launched our comprehensive Safety First Initiative, implementing industry-leading tools to protect our community members from harassment, misinformation, and harmful content.

New Safety Features:

  • Advanced AI content moderation
  • Real-time reporting and response system
  • Enhanced privacy controls for teen users
  • Collaboration with digital wellness experts

Product Updates

Version 1.2 - December 2024

New Features:

  • Enhanced Video Quality: Improved compression for better quality videos
  • Thread Discovery: New algorithm to surface interesting conversations
  • Accessibility Features: Closed captions and screen reader support
  • Creator Tools: Analytics dashboard for content creators

Bug Fixes & Improvements:

  • Fixed video upload issues on older devices
  • Improved app performance and reduced battery usage
  • Enhanced notification system
  • Better error handling and user feedback

Version 1.1 - November 2024

New Features:

  • Private Threads: Create conversations with specific friends
  • Video Reactions: React to threads with quick video responses
  • Thread Bookmarks: Save interesting conversations to revisit later
  • Dark Mode: Reduced eye strain for late-night conversations

Version 1.0 - October 2024

Launch Features:

  • 10-second video recording and sharing
  • Video thread conversations
  • User profiles and following system
  • Basic privacy controls
  • Content reporting and moderation

Company News

November 15, 2024 - New Office Opening

letsflick opened its second international office in London, UK, as part of our global expansion strategy. The London office will focus on European market development and serve as a hub for our growing international team.

August 25, 2024 - Leadership Team Expansion

We welcomed several key executives to our leadership team:

  • Sarah Chen - Chief Marketing Officer (formerly at Snapchat)
  • Michael Rodriguez - VP of Engineering (formerly at Instagram)
  • Dr. Amanda Foster - Head of Trust & Safety (formerly at Twitter)

July 10, 2024 - Community Milestone

letsflick reached 100,000 private beta users, with over 2 million video threads created during our closed testing period. The early community has shown incredible engagement, with users spending an average of 25 minutes per day in authentic video conversations.
