About Our Political Ads Library

In the interest of transparency and democratic accountability, letsflick maintains a comprehensive library of all political advertisements that have run on our platform. This resource is available to researchers, journalists, voters, and anyone interested in understanding political advertising in the digital age.

Transparency Commitment

We believe that transparency in political advertising is essential for informed democratic participation. Our ads library provides unprecedented visibility into political messaging on social media.

What Information We Provide

Advertisement Details

  • Ad Content: Full video content and transcription
  • Funding Source: Organization or individual paying for the ad
  • Spend Amount: Total amount spent on the advertisement
  • Targeting Criteria: Demographics and geographic targeting used
  • Run Dates: When the advertisement was active on the platform
  • Impressions: Number of times the ad was shown to users
  • Engagement: User interactions with the advertisement

Advertiser Information

  • Organization Name: Official name of the advertising entity
  • Contact Information: Publicly available contact details
  • Authorization Status: Verification of identity and authorization
  • Disclaimer Information: Required legal disclaimers
  • Previous Ads: Historical advertising from the same source

Campaign Context

  • Election Information: Related elections or ballot measures
  • Issue Categories: Topics addressed in the advertisement
  • Geographic Scope: Areas where the ad was shown
  • Related Content: Similar ads or campaigns

Political Advertising Requirements

Authorization Process

All political advertisers must complete our authorization process before running ads:

  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID and legal documentation
  • Organization Verification: Business registration and tax status confirmation
  • Contact Information: Valid address, phone, and email verification
  • Authorized Representative: Designated contact person for the campaign
  • Disclaimer Compliance: Agreement to include required disclaimers

Ongoing Compliance Requirements

  • Regular re-authorization during active campaign periods
  • Immediate notification of changes to organization or contact information
  • Compliance with spending limits and reporting requirements
  • Adherence to content policies and community guidelines
  • Cooperation with platform monitoring and audit processes

Disclosure Standards

  • Paid-for-by Disclaimers: Clear identification of funding source
  • Authorization Statements: Required approval language
  • Contact Information: How to reach the advertiser
  • Sponsorship Identification: Clear marking of sponsored content

Data Retention and Access

Retention Policy

  • Active Ads: Real-time availability during campaign periods

Privacy and Legal Considerations

User Privacy Protection

  • No individual user data included in ad library
  • Aggregated demographics only, no personal information
  • Targeting data shown at high level to protect user privacy
  • Compliance with all applicable privacy laws and regulations
  • Regular privacy impact assessments and updates

Legal Compliance

  • Federal Election Campaign Act (FECA) compliance for federal elections
  • State and local election laws adherence for regional campaigns
  • FCC regulations for broadcast-equivalent political advertising
  • IRS requirements for tax-exempt organization advertising
  • International standards for global political advertising transparency

Content Policies

  • All political ads subject to standard community guidelines
  • Prohibition of voter suppression or election interference content
  • Fact-checking partnerships for political claim verification
  • Rapid response protocols for problematic content
  • Clear policies on deepfakes and manipulated media
