Community Guidelines

Creating a safe and respectful space for authentic video conversations

Our Community Values

letsflick is built on the foundation of authentic self-expression and meaningful connections. We believe that everyone deserves a platform where they can be their genuine selves while feeling safe and respected.

Be Authentic

Share your real self through video threads. Authenticity creates the most meaningful conversations and connections.

What We Encourage

Positive Interactions

  • Share personal experiences and perspectives
  • Ask thoughtful questions that spark conversation
  • Respond respectfully to others' video threads
  • Celebrate diversity and different viewpoints
  • Support fellow community members

Creative Expression

  • Use your creativity to make engaging video content
  • Experiment with different topics and conversation styles
  • Share knowledge, skills, and talents
  • Start meaningful discussions about topics you care about

What We Don't Allow

Harmful Content

  • Harassment, bullying, or threats toward other users
  • Content that promotes violence or self-harm
  • Hate speech based on race, religion, gender, sexuality, or other protected characteristics
  • Sharing personal information of others without consent
  • Content that sexualizes minors

Misleading Content

  • Impersonating other people or organizations
  • Spreading false information or conspiracy theories
  • Fake accounts or coordinated inauthentic behavior
  • Spam or repetitive content

Illegal Activities

  • Promoting or depicting illegal activities
  • Sharing copyrighted content without permission
  • Selling illegal goods or services
  • Content that violates intellectual property rights

Reporting Violations

If you encounter content that violates our community guidelines, please report it immediately.

How to Report

  1. Tap the three dots (â‹¯) on any video thread
  2. Select "Report" from the menu
  3. Choose the reason for reporting
  4. Provide additional details if needed
  5. Submit your report

We Take Action

All reports are reviewed by our safety team within 24 hours. We may remove content, issue warnings, or suspend accounts based on the severity of violations.

Consequences for Violations

First-Time Violations

  • Content removal and written warning
  • Educational resources about community guidelines
  • Temporary restriction on posting (24-48 hours)

Repeated Violations

  • Extended suspension from posting (1-7 days)
  • Restriction of certain platform features
  • Account review and potential permanent suspension

Severe Violations

  • Immediate account suspension
  • Permanent ban from the platform
  • Report to law enforcement when applicable

Appeals Process

If you believe your content was removed or your account was suspended in error, you can appeal our decision.

How to Appeal

  1. Go to your account settings
  2. Select "Appeal a Decision"
  3. Provide details about why you believe the action was incorrect
  4. Submit supporting evidence if available
  5. Wait for our review team to respond (usually within 3-5 business days)

Staying Safe

Your safety is our priority. Here are some tips to protect yourself on letsflick:

  • Don't share personal information like your address, phone number, or financial details
  • Use the block feature if someone makes you uncomfortable
  • Be cautious about meeting people from the platform in person
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, report it
  • Keep your account secure with a strong password
