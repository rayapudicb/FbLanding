Advertising Policies

Guidelines and requirements for advertising on letsflick

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

These advertising policies govern all promotional content on letsflick, including paid advertisements, sponsored content, and creator partnerships. All advertisers must comply with these policies to maintain a safe and trustworthy platform for our community.

Our Advertising Philosophy

We believe advertising should enhance the user experience, not detract from it. All ads on letsflick should be authentic, relevant, and respectful of our community.

General Advertising Standards

Truthfulness and Accuracy

  • All advertising claims must be truthful, accurate, and substantiated
  • Misleading or deceptive content is strictly prohibited
  • Products and services must be accurately represented
  • Testimonials must be genuine and verifiable
  • Pricing and promotional terms must be clearly stated

Content Quality Requirements

  • High-quality video content with clear audio and visual elements
  • Professional presentation that meets platform standards
  • Appropriate content that aligns with community guidelines
  • Clear branding and sponsor identification
  • Engaging content that provides value to viewers

Disclosure Requirements

  • All sponsored content must be clearly labeled as advertising
  • Creator partnerships must include #ad or #sponsored hashtags
  • Material connections between advertisers and creators must be disclosed
  • Promotional offers and terms must be clearly stated
  • Any paid relationships must be transparent to users

Prohibited Content

Illegal Products and Services

  • Illegal drugs, controlled substances, or drug paraphernalia
  • Weapons, ammunition, or explosive devices
  • Counterfeit goods or intellectual property violations
  • Illegal gambling or unregulated financial services
  • Any products or services that violate local, state, or federal laws

Harmful or Dangerous Content

  • Content promoting violence, self-harm, or dangerous activities
  • Advertisements for tobacco products or smoking accessories
  • Content that could cause physical or mental harm
  • Promotion of eating disorders or dangerous weight loss methods
  • Content that exploits tragedy, conflict, or sensitive events

Inappropriate or Offensive Content

  • Adult content, sexual services, or explicit material
  • Discriminatory content based on protected characteristics
  • Hate speech or content targeting individuals or groups
  • Profanity, vulgar language, or inappropriate imagery
  • Content that promotes harassment or bullying

Misleading Practices

  • False claims about product benefits or effectiveness
  • Fake testimonials or manufactured social proof
  • Hidden fees, terms, or conditions
  • Bait-and-switch advertising tactics
  • Impersonation of other brands or public figures

Restricted Content Categories

Financial Services

  • Allowed: Banks, credit unions, established investment firms
  • Restricted: Cryptocurrency, get-rich-quick schemes, payday loans
  • Requirements: Appropriate licensing, clear terms and disclosures

Health and Wellness

  • Allowed: General fitness, wellness products with FDA approval
  • Restricted: Weight loss supplements, medical devices, prescription drugs
  • Requirements: Scientific substantiation, appropriate disclaimers

Alcohol

  • Allowed: Beer, wine, spirits (21+ targeting only)
  • Restricted: Excessive drinking promotion, targeting minors
  • Requirements: Age-appropriate targeting, responsible consumption messaging

Political Content

  • Allowed: Issue advocacy, civic engagement campaigns
  • Restricted: Candidate campaigns during election periods
  • Requirements: Transparency requirements, disclosure of funding sources

Technical Requirements

Video Specifications

  • Format: MP4, MOV, or AVI
  • Aspect Ratio: 9:16 (vertical) or 1:1 (square) preferred
  • Duration: 3-15 seconds for standard ads, up to 60 seconds for sponsored content
  • File Size: Maximum 100MB
  • Resolution: Minimum 720p, 1080p recommended

Audio Requirements

  • Clear, high-quality audio without distortion
  • Background music should not overpower spoken content
  • Captions or subtitles recommended for accessibility
  • Audio levels should be consistent throughout

Visual Standards

  • Sharp, well-lit footage without excessive blur or grain
  • Text overlays should be legible and appropriately sized
  • Brand logos and elements should be clearly visible
  • Color contrast sufficient for accessibility compliance

Targeting and Data Use

Audience Targeting Guidelines

  • Age-appropriate targeting for all content
  • Respect user privacy preferences and opt-out requests
  • No targeting based on sensitive personal information
  • Compliance with applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Transparent data collection and usage practices

Prohibited Targeting Practices

  • Discrimination based on protected characteristics
  • Targeting minors for inappropriate content
  • Using sensitive health data without proper consent
  • Targeting based on financial vulnerabilities
  • Creating "dark patterns" to manipulate user behavior

Data Protection Requirements

  • Secure handling of all user data and advertising metrics
  • Clear privacy policies and data usage disclosures
  • User consent for data collection where required by law
  • Right to access, correct, or delete personal information
  • Regular security audits and data protection assessments

Creator Partnership Policies

Sponsored Content Requirements

  • Clear disclosure of paid partnerships in video and description
  • Honest opinions and authentic creator voice maintained
  • Products must be personally used or genuinely endorsed
  • No false claims about creator relationships with brands
  • Compliance with FTC guidelines for endorsements

Creator Responsibility

  • Creators must disclose material connections with brands
  • Content must align with creator's usual style and audience
  • No deceptive practices or hidden commercial intent
  • Respect for community guidelines and platform policies
  • Timely delivery of contracted content and performance metrics

Brand Partnership Standards

  • Authentic alignment between brand values and creator audience
  • Clear contracts outlining deliverables and expectations
  • Respect for creator editorial independence and authenticity
  • Fair compensation and transparent partnership terms
  • No pressure for creators to violate platform policies

Enforcement and Compliance

Review Process

  • All advertisements undergo automated and manual review
  • Review typically completed within 24-48 hours
  • Complex or sensitive content may require additional review time
  • Ongoing monitoring of live campaigns for policy compliance
  • User reporting system for policy violations

Violations and Consequences

Minor Violations:

  • Content removal and revision requirement
  • Educational resources about policy compliance
  • Warning notification to advertiser account

Serious Violations:

  • Campaign suspension and account review
  • Requirement for policy training before resuming advertising
  • Enhanced review requirements for future campaigns

Severe or Repeated Violations:

  • Permanent advertising ban from the platform
  • Forfiture of remaining advertising credits
  • Potential legal action for fraudulent or illegal content

Appeals Process

  • Advertisers may appeal policy decisions within 30 days
  • Submit appeals through advertiser support channel
  • Include detailed explanation and supporting documentation
  • Appeals reviewed by senior policy team within 5 business days
  • Final decisions communicated with reasoning provided

Special Considerations

International Advertising

  • Compliance with local laws and regulations in target markets
  • Cultural sensitivity and appropriate localization
  • Currency and pricing transparency for international audiences
  • Language accuracy and professional translation quality
  • Respect for local customs and social norms

Seasonal and Event-Based Content

  • Appropriate timing and context for seasonal promotions
  • Sensitivity around holidays and cultural celebrations
  • Respectful approach to current events and news
  • No exploitation of tragedies or sensitive situations for commercial gain

Emerging Technologies

  • AR/VR advertising content must be clearly labeled
  • AI-generated content must be disclosed appropriately
  • New advertising formats subject to updated policy guidelines
  • Continuous evaluation of emerging advertising technologies

Resources and Support

Advertiser Education

  • Best practices guides and creative inspiration
  • Webinars and training sessions on policy compliance
  • One-on-one consultation for complex campaigns
  • Regular updates on policy changes and new requirements

Policy Support

  • Policy Questions: adpolicy@letsflick.com
  • Compliance Review: Available before campaign launch
  • Appeals: appeals@letsflick.com
  • Training Resources: Available in advertiser dashboard

Industry Guidelines

letsflick's advertising policies align with and exceed industry standards, including:

  • Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) guidelines
  • Federal Trade Commission (FTC) endorsement guidelines
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) privacy principles
  • Global Alliance for Responsible Media (GARM) standards

Policy Updates

Change Notification Process

  • 30-day advance notice for major policy changes
  • Email notifications to all active advertisers
  • In-dashboard alerts and educational resources
  • Grace period for existing campaigns to come into compliance

Staying Current

  • Subscribe to policy updates: adpolicy-updates@letsflick.com
  • Follow @letsflickbusiness for policy announcements
  • Regular review of advertiser dashboard for notifications
  • Participation in advertiser community forums

Contact Policy Team

Questions about specific advertising requirements?

Our policy team is available to provide guidance on compliance requirements, review campaigns before launch, and help ensure your advertising content meets our standards.

These advertising policies are designed to maintain a safe, trustworthy environment for our community while providing effective marketing opportunities for businesses. We reserve the right to make exceptions or additional requirements based on individual campaign circumstances.
