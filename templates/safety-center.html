Safety Center

Your safety and security on letsflick are our top priorities

Safety Tools & Features

letsflick provides several tools to help you control your experience and stay safe while using the platform.

Block & Report

Quickly block users who make you uncomfortable and report content that violates our community guidelines.

Privacy Controls

Account Privacy Settings

  • Private Account: Make your profile private so only approved followers can see your videos
  • Comments Control: Choose who can reply to your video threads
  • Discovery Settings: Control whether your videos appear in recommended feeds
  • Contact Settings: Limit who can send you direct messages

Video Privacy Options

  • Public: Anyone can see and respond to your video threads
  • Friends Only: Only your approved connections can view your content
  • Draft Mode: Save videos privately before deciding to share
  • Time-Limited: Set videos to automatically delete after 24 hours

Digital Wellbeing

Managing Screen Time

  • Set daily time limits for app usage
  • Enable break reminders every 30 minutes
  • Use "Focus Mode" to limit distracting notifications
  • Schedule quiet hours when you won't receive notifications

Content Filtering

  • Hide videos with specific keywords or topics
  • Filter out content from accounts you've muted
  • Adjust content sensitivity settings
  • Control what appears in your recommended feed

Take Regular Breaks

Remember to take breaks from social media to maintain your mental health and well-being. Consider setting app limits or using our built-in break reminders.

Protecting Your Information

What We Collect

  • Account information (email, username, profile details)
  • Video content and engagement data
  • Device and technical information
  • Usage patterns and preferences

How We Protect Your Data

  • End-to-end encryption for all video content
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis

Your Data Rights

  • Download a copy of your data
  • Delete specific videos or your entire account
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Control how your data is used for recommendations

Safety for Teens

We provide additional protections for users under 18 years old.

Teen-Specific Features

  • Accounts are automatically set to private for users under 16
  • Limited contact from adult users they don't know
  • Restricted ability to make purchases
  • Enhanced content filtering and moderation
  • Additional privacy protections for personal information

For Parents and Guardians

  • Family safety tools to monitor teen accounts
  • Time management features for healthy usage
  • Educational resources about social media safety
  • Direct communication with our safety team

Parental Controls

Parents can access additional safety features through our Family Safety Dashboard. Contact us at family@letsflick.com to learn more.

Crisis Resources

If you or someone you know is experiencing a crisis, help is available.

Mental Health Support

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • SAMHSA National Helpline: 1-800-662-4357
  • Trevor Project (LGBTQ Youth): 1-866-488-7386

Safety Emergency

  • Emergency Services: 911
  • National Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-7233
  • RAINN Sexual Assault Hotline: 1-800-656-4673
  • National Child Abuse Hotline: 1-800-4-A-CHILD

Reporting Emergencies

If you encounter content depicting immediate danger or illegal activity:

  1. Contact local emergency services immediately (911)
  2. Report the content to us through in-app reporting
  3. Save screenshots or evidence if safe to do so
  4. Email us at emergency@letsflick.com with details

We're Here to Help

Our safety team monitors reports 24/7 and works with law enforcement when necessary to ensure user safety.

Stay Informed

Safety is an ongoing effort. Here's how to stay updated:

  • Follow @letsficksafety for safety tips and updates
  • Check our Safety Center regularly for new resources
  • Attend our monthly virtual safety workshops
  • Subscribe to safety alerts and platform updates

Read Community Guidelines

Need immediate help? Contact our safety team at safety@letsflick.com or through the in-app emergency report feature.
