Creative Best Practices

Video Content Guidelines

  • Authenticity First: Create content that feels natural and genuine
  • Hook Early: Capture attention in the first 2-3 seconds
  • Clear Branding: Include brand elements without overwhelming the message
  • Call to Action: Include a clear, compelling next step
  • Mobile-Optimized: Ensure content looks great on mobile devices

Platform-Specific Tips

  • Use vertical or square video formats
  • Include captions for accessibility
  • Keep text overlay minimal and readable
  • Encourage replies and engagement
  • Test different creative approaches
